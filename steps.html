So you've looked into booking your trip, what's next?

At Blastoff, we have some steps to get you ready for your trip: preparation, planning, and safety. After all of these steps are taken, it's time for you to take off!

Preparation:

We require various levels of training to best prepare you for space. As it willl be different from Earth, we do our best to simulate what you may experience once out of orbit. Click the button to learn more about the steps you will take to prepare, along with our requirements for going to space.

Planning:

So you're ready to dive into space travel... let's book your trip! Start looking at our vacation packages as well as our various activities you can experience. Then head over to our 'Book a Trip' page to plan your intergalactic journey! We can't wait to accompany your once in a lifetime trip!

Safety:

Now that you're all ready to go to space, we have to get you briefed on our safety protocals as well as our general guidelines. Click this button for our orientation!
